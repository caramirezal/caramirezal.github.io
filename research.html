Research

My work is focused in understanding the process of gene expression regulation using different biological models with medical interest

Experience in dynamic regulatory network models applied to the simulation of the differentiation of granulocytes and monocytes, which are blood cells

I have also worked as bioinformatic consultant for the implementation of pipelines for the analysis of polymorphisms associated to sport, nutrition and health

As a Postdoctoral researcher I analyse single cell Sequencing data from different molecular levels like mRNA and chromating accesible regions
